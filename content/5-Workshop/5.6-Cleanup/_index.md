## CLEAN UP

#### Clean up resources

To avoid incurring future charges, you should unsubscribe from QuickSight if you do not plan to use it further.

1. **Manage QuickSight**:
   - Click on your user icon in the top right corner.
   - Choose **Manage QuickSight**.

2. **Account Settings**:
   - Go to **Account settings**.
   - Choose **Delete account**.
   - Type `delete` to confirm.
   - Choose **Delete account**.

---

**Note:** *If you are on the Free Trial, you will not be charged until the trial ends, but it is good practice to clean up if you are done.*
